Repair Order = RO

RO: A repair order is a document that contains all of the customer and instrument info. It also records any of the customer requests for service or repairs, and that is what is used throughout the entire process.

Please note all repair times given are an approximation; it will never be exact. When working with wood and finishes there are a lot of external factors that can warrant an extension of repair time, please account for this in your planning.

The Founder

Jessie Baker

I played music professionally for over a decade and I always relied on other folks to repair and maintain my instruments.

After I retired from the road full-time, the luthier to whom I'd trusted my instruments with since long before I was a professional, sadly passed away. The thought of taking my instruments to anyone else wasn't an option; I decided to learn to repair and build myself.

I have set-up and repaired instruments for some of the best players out there and I'm thankful that they trust their instruments to me.
